How to Use ShopEase

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to navigate our website:

Visit the Products page to explore all available categories such as Men's, Women's, Kids, Accessories, and Beauty. You can use the filter options on the left to narrow down your search based on your interests.

If you know what you are looking for, simply type the product name in the Search Bar at the top of the page. Our system will display relevant results as you type.

Once you find a product you want to purchase, click the "Add to Cart" button on the product page. You can review your cart at any time by clicking the cart icon in the top-right corner.

When you're ready to purchase, click the Checkout button. You’ll be asked to provide your shipping information and payment details.

To complete your purchase, you will need to either sign up for a new account or log in to your existing account.
